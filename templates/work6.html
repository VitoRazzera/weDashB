006 : Backorders

This page will display information such as the number of orders that could not be fulfilled due to missing stock, number of customers affected by the stockouts and the revenue lost as a result. The dashboard may help identify trends and patterns in stockouts, such as which products are frequently out of stock or which suppliers have the most delays in restocking.

This information could be used to optimize inventory management and improve the accuracy of stock forecasting.
